NodePort under ubuntu:
- minikube start
- eval $(minikube docker-env)
- minikube tunnel
- kubectl describe service serviceName
- check NodePort
- kubectl get nodes -o wide
- get an internal IP and add NodePort to access it 
example: http://172.17.0.2:30402/posts
